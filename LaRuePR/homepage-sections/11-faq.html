What is the difference between PR, influencer marketing, and affiliate marketing — and do I need all three?+
PR builds credibility through earned media, influencer marketing activates trusted voices to drive consideration, and affiliate marketing connects it all to measurable revenue. The brands growing fastest aren't choosing between them, they're running all three as one integrated strategy.
What is AI/Generative Engine Optimization (AEO/GEO) and why does it matter for my brand in 2026?+
AEO/GEO is the practice of ensuring your brand gets cited and recommended by AI tools like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews: the new way people discover and decide on brands. If you're not optimizing for it, you're invisible to a growing portion of your audience.
How do I get my brand recommended by ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews?+
AI tools recommend brands that have consistent earned media coverage, a clear and authoritative brand narrative, and content that directly answers the questions your audience is asking.
How long does it take to see results from a PR campaign?+
Media placements can start appearing within the first 30 to 60 days, but meaningful brand authority builds over time. The brands that get frustrated with PR are usually the ones measuring it too narrowly, too soon.
How do I measure the ROI of PR beyond press clips and impressions?+
The metrics that actually matter are branded search volume, inbound lead quality, AI citation frequency, and conversion rate changes on pages that receive PR-driven traffic.
