FAQ

Everything you want to know about LaRue.

Choosing a PR agency is a big decision. Our full FAQ covers how we work, what the first 90 days look like, how we measure success, and what makes LaRue the right fit for growth-stage brands.

Read Our Full FAQ →
How do you measure PR success?+
We measure success against the KPIs that actually move your business — media impressions, share of voice, affiliate-attributed revenue, press-driven conversions, and brand search lift. Every engagement starts with defining what "winning" looks like for your brand, then reporting against it monthly.
What does the first 90 days look like?+
The first 30 days are onboarding: deep-dive into your brand, competitor audit, building target media lists, and drafting foundational press materials. Days 31–60 we go live with active outreach. Days 61–90 you should see placements land and we iterate based on what's resonating.
What industries do you specialize in?+
Our deepest roots are in fashion, beauty, wellness, food & beverage, home & lifestyle, and consumer tech — particularly DTC and emerging brands. That said, our media relationships and affiliate infrastructure work across verticals, and we regularly take on category-defying clients.
Do you work with early-stage brands?+
Yes — we work with brands at every stage, from pre-launch to scale. Our offerings are structured so founders can start with a focused scope (e.g., launch PR only) and expand into full-service as the brand grows.
What's included in a typical retainer?+
Retainers typically include a dedicated account team, proactive media outreach, reactive pitch coverage, affiliate program management (if applicable), monthly reporting, and access to our senior strategists. Scope is customized to your goals — ask us for a sample scope for your stage.
